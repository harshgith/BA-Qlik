    Objectives of our Project

    1. Revenue Optimization - Airline wants to optimize revenue by analyzing historical ticket sales data,identifying peak travel times, popular destinations.
    2. Operational Efficiency - Airline wants to improve operational efficiency by analyzing flight delays, cancelled rates, passenger flows.
    3. Customer Experience Enhancement - Airline wants to enhance customer experience by analyzing customer feedback,satisfaction levels.
